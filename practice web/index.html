Document Required
  1. A self-attested copy of birth certificate of the child along with original for verification. The certificate issued by Municipal Corporation only is valid.
  2. One Passport Size Photograph of Father, Mother and the child is mandatory.
  3. Photocopy of Caste certificate if SC/ST/OBC along with Original for verification (If applicable).
  4. Original bonafide certificate is compulsory at the time of registration.
  5. Transfer Certificate of current school (compulsory for admission to classes II onwards). TC from the previous school must be submitted within 15 days after the commencement of the academic session.
  6. A copy of previous year report card / mid-term report card.
  7. Original medical fitness certificate issued by MBBS/ MD doctor is mandatory.
  8. A self-attested copy of Aadhar Card of the Student and both the parents along with Original for Verification (not mandatory).
  9. A self-attested copy of PAN Card / Driving License / passport of both the parents along with Original for verification. This is required to verify the signatures on admission form.
  10. In case of death of any one parent copy of death certificate is mandatory.
Please bring the original documents to be verified at the time of submission of the photocopies.
